### User data format ###

Hint:
 1. Blank line is ignored
 2. text after '#' on a line is ignored
 3. Load sample and see how it's done.

 --------------------------------------------------------------
Format:
--------------------------------------------------------------
[number of users] (up to 4 users)
[1st user's name] (up to 10 letters)
[1st user's current stage] (make this 0 to play from the start)
[1st user's reward key] (4 letters)
[2nd user's name] 
[2nd user's current stage]
[2nd user's reward key]
  ... up to 4 users

--------------------------------------------------------------
Example:
--------------------------------------------------------------
1
George
0
1234
